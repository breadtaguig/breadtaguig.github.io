MCGI Bible Readers participated in the Flag Ceremony at Taguig City Hall, led by the SK Federation and the Local Youth Development Office (LYDO), August 11. The group was honored to lead the invocation, lifting up the city and its youth in faith and unity. 

After the ceremony, they also joined the kickoff of the Taguig Youth Bazaar, marking the beginning of Linggo ng Kabataan and the celebration of International Youth Day.
