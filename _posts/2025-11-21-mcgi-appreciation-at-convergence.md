Members Church of God International (MCGI), together with the MCGI Bible Readers, proudly participated in the 2025 Stakeholders' Convergence & Partners’ Appreciation Day held at the Taguig City University Auditorium on November 21, 2025.

MCGI was honored with a Plaque of Appreciation for its unwavering support and meaningful contribution as a Schools Division partner in the successful implementation of the Adopt-a-School Program.

Thanks be to God and to God be the glory!
